About Certificate Forgery Crisis

In recent years, Uganda has experienced a rise in forged academic certificates. Employers have unknowingly hired individuals with fake qualifications, causing financial losses and damaging organisational reputation.

Steps to Verify a Certificate Through UNEB

  1. Collect a copy of the applicant's academic certificate.
  2. Comfirm the candidate's full names and index number.
  3. Visit the UNEB verification portal or nearest examination centre.
  4. Submit the certificate details for verification.
  5. Pay the reqired vetrification fee if applicable.
  6. Wait for official confirmation from UNEB.

Documents Verified by UNEB

  • UCE Certificates
  • UACE Certificates
  • Diplomas
  • Degree Certificates
  • Academic Transcripts
  • Professional Certificates

UNEB Public Announcement

The Uganda National Examinations Board has intensified efforts to fight against academic fraud across the country. Employers are encouraged to verify all academic documents before hiring candidates.

UNEB has introduced a digital verification system and regional support centres to ensure faster and more reliable services for the public.
